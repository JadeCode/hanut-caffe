Hanut Caffe

In a quiet street in the center of Rehovot there is a little shop, with yellow walls and sounds of Jazz. But as soon you enter and you take the first breath, you are somewhere else, in Ethiopia or Honduras or perhaps in Indonesia.
You have entered a shop where you can get fresh coffee, grounded to every method of preparing, and a good cup of coffee. In our shop you may choose either a single origin coffee, which means it was picked from a single plantation, roasted to perfection and poured especially for you with no other stops in between, or to have a blend we make ourselves from the single origin coffees from a recipe that was made by the store owner Dorit Al

The Coffee Plant

It all starts in the rainy Amazons where the coffe plant strives. As you can see the beans do not ripen at the same time, which means harvesting the ripe beans is a year round job.

Methods of Making Coffee

  • "Black Coffee"

    Making Black, or Turkish or cooking coffee: it means you take the finest grinded coffee and make it soak in hot water fora few minutes. The coffee will extract most of the Caffeine and will have a more mellow taste than an espresso.
