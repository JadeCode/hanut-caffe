In a quiet street in the center of Rehovot there is a little shop, with yellow walls and Jazz playing. But as soon you enter and take the first breath, you are suddenly somewhere else, in Ethiopia or Honduras or perhaps in Indonesia.

You have entered a shop where you can get a bag of fresh coffee, ground to your specific tastes and needs, as well as a good cup of coffee. In our shop you may choose either a single origin coffee, or to have a blend we make ourselves from the single origin coffees from a recipe that was made by the store owner Dorit Aldema.
